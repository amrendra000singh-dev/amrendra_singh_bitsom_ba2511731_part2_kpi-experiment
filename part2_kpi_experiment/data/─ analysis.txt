# Business Problem Statement

## Decision to be Made
Should we launch the new campaign across our entire user base?

## Who is Impacted?
Our existing and potential customers, the marketing department (budget allocation, campaign strategy), the sales team (lead quality, conversion rates), and ultimately the company's revenue and profitability.

## Metric to Improve
The primary goal of the new campaign is to significantly improve the **Paid Conversion Rate**.

## Risks to Monitor
- **Increased churn rate**: The new campaign might attract users who are less loyal or increase dissatisfaction among existing users.
- **Negative brand perception**: The campaign messaging or execution could lead to negative sentiment or damage brand image.
- **Increased support ticket volume**: New users or changes introduced by the campaign might lead to confusion and a surge in customer support inquiries.
- **Reduced Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: While conversion might increase, the quality of converted users or their spending habits might decrease.
